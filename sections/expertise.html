01 · Regulatory Compliance

KYC, AML & AMLR 2027

The EU Anti-Money Laundering Regulation enters force on 10 July 2027, replacing fragmented national directives with a single rulebook. We help obliged entities prepare, from gap analysis and CDD redesign to beneficial ownership transparency and AMLA readiness.

03 · Digital Identity

eIDAS 2.0 & EUDI Wallet

By late 2026, every EU Member State must offer a digital identity wallet to citizens. Companies in the financial sector will need to accept them from 2027 onward. We actively follow these developments and their implications for KYC and onboarding flows, so we can advise our clients on what is coming and how to stay ahead as the new trust framework takes shape.

Key Dates

Jul 2027

Jan 2028

AMLA begins direct supervision of high-risk cross-border financial institutions across the EU.
